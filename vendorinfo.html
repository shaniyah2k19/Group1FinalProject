Vendor Overview

Freeze Stop Vending Solutions is a leading provider of innovative vending machines specializing in freezer packs. With a commitment to delivering convenience and refreshment, our state-of-the-art machines offer a quick and efficient solution for those in need of instant cooling relief. Whether you're at the beach, on a road trip, or simply looking to beat the heat, Freeze Stop has you covered with our freezer packs vending machines.


Product Line

  • Frosty Cubes: Our signature freezer pack, Frosty Cubes, are perfect for keeping beverages, snacks, and lunches cool on the go. Compact and reusable, they provide a quick and easy cooling solution for any occasion.

  • Frosty Packs: Specially designed for perishable items, Frosty Packs are larger freezer packs ideal for keeping perishable goods fresh and safe. Perfect for picnics, outdoor events, and camping trips.

  • Frosty Wraps: Quench your thirst with Frost Wraps, our innovative frozen drink sleeves. Simply slip one onto your favorite beverage, and enjoy an icy-cold experience that lasts.
