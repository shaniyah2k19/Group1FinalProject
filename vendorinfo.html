Vendor Overview

Frost Stop Vending Solutions is a leading provider of innovative vending machines specializing in freezer packs. With a commitment to delivering convenience and refreshment, our state-of-the-art machines offer a quick and efficient solution for those in need of INSTANT COOLING RELIEF. Whether you're at the beach, on a road trip, or simply looking to beat the heat, Frost Stop has you covered with our freezer packs vending machines.


FROSTY CUBES

Perfect for keeping beverages, snacks, and lunches cool on the go. Compact and reusable, they provide a quick and easy cooling solution for any occasion. Sold in quantities of 4 cubes per pack.

FROSTY PACKS

Larger freezer packs ideal for keeping perishable goods fresh and safe. Perfect for picnics, outdoor events, and camping trips.

FROSTY WRAPS

Quench your thirst at any time with our innovative frozen drink sleeves. Simply slip one onto your favorite beverage, and enjoy an icy-cold experience that lasts.
